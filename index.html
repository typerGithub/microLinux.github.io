About microLinux

microLinux is a lightweight Linux distribution designed to run on ESP32 microcontrollers. It brings the power of Linux to embedded systems, providing a flexible environment for developers and hobbyists.

Features

  • Compact and optimized for ESP32.
  • Supports basic Linux commands and scripting.
  • Open-source and community-driven.
  • Compatible with a variety of peripherals and sensors.

Getting Started

  1. Download the microLinux firmware for ESP32 from the official repository.
  2. Flash the firmware to your ESP32 using your preferred tool.
  3. Connect to the microLinux shell via UART or SSH.
  4. Start exploring the Linux environment on your microcontroller!
